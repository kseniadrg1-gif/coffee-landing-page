Our Philosophy

Coffee deserves to be
understood, not just sipped

We designed a 12-day itinerary through Ethiopia's most legendary coffee regions — Yirgacheffe, Jimma, and Harar — so you can trace every note in your cup back to the soil it grew in.

You'll meet the farmers, cup at the washing stations, and leave with a deep understanding of terroir, process, and culture. No coffee knowledge required.

The Itinerary

Days 1–2

Addis Ababa

Arrival, city tour, Tomoca Coffee, Merkato spice market, welcome dinner.

Days 3–5

Yirgacheffe

Visit legendary washing stations, hike through wild coffee forests, daily cuppings.

Days 6–8

Jimma

Kaffa Forest — birthplace of coffee, meet local farmers, forest bathing.

Days 9–10

Harar

Ancient walled city, dry-processed naturals, hyena feeding ceremony at dusk.

Days 11–12

Return

Final cupping competition, farewell dinner, flight home with 3 kg of sourced beans.
